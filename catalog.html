Baguio Botanical Garden

Description:

The Baguio Botanical Garden, famously known as Imelda Park is a botanical park in Baguio City known for it's big pine trees, colorful displays of flowers, and lush greenery. This park offers a peaceful spot to stroll and relax.

37 Leonard Wood Rd, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Baguio Burnham Park

Description:

The Baguio City: Burnham Park is one of the most iconic and beloved attractions tourism in the City. Named after American architect, Daniel Burnham, who built and designed the city's layout, this park offers tourists a scenic spot for picnics, boat rides, biking, and relaxing strolls in the beautiful gardens.

Jose Abad Santos Dr, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Baguio City SM

Description:

Baguio City SM is a popular shopping mall that offers a wide range of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities. It is the 22nd largest shopping mall in the Philippines.

SM City Baguio, Luneta Hill Dr, 231, Baguio, 2600 Benguet

Yellow Trail

Description:

The Yellow Trail is a popular hiking trail in Baguio City that offers scenic views of the surrounding mountains and forests. It is a great way to experience the natural beauty of the area and get some exercise at the same time.

Camp John Hay, Baguio, 2600 Benguet
